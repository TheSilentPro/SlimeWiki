/item <name> Retrieve information on an item.
/recipe <item> Retrieve the recipe for an item.
/research <key> Retrieve information on a research.
/group <name> Retrieve information on a group (category).
/player <name> Retrieve information on a player.
/items [type] [page] Retrieve a list of all items for the specified type.
/groups [page] Retrieve a list of all groups (categories)
/researches [page] Retrieve a list of all researches.
/getid [channel] Retrieve an id.
